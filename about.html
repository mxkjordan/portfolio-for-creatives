  • Jordan Mak

    Hello! I’m Jordan! I’m a Chinese-Canadian UX/UI designer, born and raised in Vancouver, BC. I’m currently studying Interaction Design at Capilano University’s IDEA School of Design. I spend a lot of my time working with youth-led organizations, and I’m currently the Director of Outreach for Momentum Case Competition. I enjoy helping others succeed and supporting teams in reaching their goals. Being a director in a youth organization might not seem like a big role at first, but I put a lot of time and care into making sure my peers are happy with the work I contribute. Working at the youth organizations is different like working a part-time job, but just using my free time to help with other organizations. I worked as a graphic designer with Horizon Publications, event director with Open Minds Foundation, and Director of Outreach with Momentum Case Competition operated by Open Minds Foundation. Outside of design, I’m a K-pop fan, dancer. When it comes to design, I aim to bring a fresh and thoughtful perspective. I always strive to create work that is accessible, stylish, and user-friendly. I’m a passionate designer who always puts my best work forward.
